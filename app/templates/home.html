How to Register

Join any school event in just a few simple steps. No complicated process, just pure excitement.

01

Browse Events

Explore all upcoming events on our portal. Filter by type, date, or status to find what interests you.

02

Read Details

Click on any event to view full details — venue, time, organizer, description, and seat availability.

03

Fill the Form

Enter your name, class, section, roll number and email to complete your registration instantly.

04

You're Registered!

Get instant confirmation and show up on the event day. We'll see you there!
